About Premium Automation

Founded in 2015 by a team of marketing technologists and data scientists, Premium Automation emerged from a simple observation: businesses were spending enormous amounts on marketing tools that didn't talk to each other, and their teams were drowning in manual processes. Our founders, having spent years in the trenches at leading marketing departments, decided to build the intelligent automation platform they always wanted. What started as a passionate side project quickly evolved into a comprehensive solution that has helped thousands of companies transform their marketing operations and achieve unprecedented growth. Today, Premium Automation serves companies ranging from ambitious startups to Fortune 500 enterprises across 45 countries.

Our mission is simple yet powerful: to democratize advanced marketing automation and artificial intelligence, making it accessible to businesses of all sizes. We believe that every company deserves access to enterprise-grade marketing technology that drives real results. We're committed to continuous innovation, unwavering customer success, and maintaining the highest standards of data privacy and security. Our core values—transparency, excellence, customer-centricity, and ethical AI—guide every decision we make. We're not just building software; we're building the future of marketing, one customer success story at a time.
